Adobe Photoshop

Previous Next

CSI-SPIT, student branch organized a hands-on workshop on Adobe Photoshop, a professional photo-editing software. It was conducted by Kapil Gadhire and Viswesh Sivaraman, the students of BE IT and Ronak Parpani, a student of TE Computers. There were a total of 88 participants. The participants were introduced to the basic tools and their general usage. Once everyone had mastered the basics, some special features were explained in detail. Interesting examples of graphics editing were discussed. The participants were enthusiastic in the learning process and at the end of the day were inspired to explore their creativity. The workshop was very informative and also very enjoyable. A positive feedback was received.
